Red Velvet

Red velvet cake is much more than vanilla cake tinted red.

This recipe produces the best red velvet cake with superior buttery, vanilla, and cocoa flavors, as well as a delicious tang from buttermilk. My trick is to whip the egg whites, which guarantees a smooth velvet crumb.

Ingredients

  • 2Cake Flour
  • Unsweetened Cocoa Powder
  • butter and oil

Steps

  1. Put the flour and cocoa in a bowl and stir briefly.
  2. Add butter and oil.
